1. Simulation Parameters
To verify the theoretical outcomes of the bridge rectifier, the following parameters were configured in LTspice:                                                            AC Voltage Source ($V_{in}$): 100V Peak Amplitude, 50 Hz Frequency.                   Simulation Type: Transient Analysis (.tran).                                          Stop Time: 40ms (Calculated as $2 \times \frac{1}{f} = 2 \times 20\text{ms}$ to capture two full cycles).                                                              Components: 4 Diodes (Bridge configuration), Load Resistor ($R_L$), and Filter Capacitor ($C$).                                                                       2. Circuit Analysis & Behavior
Part A: Without Capacitor (Pulsating DC)
The plot shows that the bridge rectifier flips the negative half-cycles of the AC input into the positive domain.
