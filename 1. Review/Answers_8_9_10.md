## Question 8: Electricity vs. Electronics
* **Electricity:** Deals with the flow of energy to power systems using conductors and passive components (resistors, inductors).
* **Electronics:** Deals with the control of vacuum or semiconductor devices to process information or data.
* **Difference:** Electricity is about power transmission; Electronics is about signal control.

## Question 9: Electronic Device - Smartphone
A smartphone uses **Transistors** in its CPU to perform logic operations, **Capacitors** to filter power supply noise, and **Diodes** for the screen display.

## Question 10: Scope and Applications
* **Electricity:** Power plants, home wiring, industrial motors.
* **Electronics:** Computing, telecommunications, medical robotics.
